Yes. Every lesson was created from scratch for the January 2026 TOEFL iBT — including all seven new question types. It is not old material with a small update.
How many lessons are there?
86 focused lessons across five modules (Introduction, Listening, Reading, Speaking, Writing), about 8.2 hours of teaching, plus 16 Module-End Skill Tests, audio, and downloadable PDFs.
What's the difference between the three plans?
All three include the full course and 16 Skill Tests. Self-Study ($79) is the complete course on its own. Practice Plus ($199) adds teacher Speaking & Writing reviews, a study plan, and a weakness report. Guided Sprint ($499) adds up to 5 private 1:1 checkpoints, more reviews, homework direction, and exam-week strategy. All are one-time payments.
Is this a subscription?
No. Every Aidoss plan is a one-time payment with course access — never a monthly subscription.
Are the Mock Exams included?
The 20 Complete Timed Mock Exams (Reading, Listening, Speaking, Writing) are sold separately. Each plan includes a credit toward them or the Aidoss Library ($25 / $50 / $100). The Target Score Intensive includes all 20.
I need a specific target score — what should I take?
Start with Guided Sprint if you want guided progress, or apply for the Target Score Intensive — a private, application-only plan built around your exam date and target after a free diagnostic. We set a realistic target together; we don't promise guaranteed scores.
