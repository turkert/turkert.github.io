Frequently asked questions

Is this really built for the new 2026 TOEFL format?
Yes. Every lesson was created from scratch for the January 2026 TOEFL iBT — including all seven new question types (Complete the Words, Listen & Repeat, Take an Interview, Build-a-Sentence, Email, Academic Discussion and more). It is not an old course with an "update" bolted on.
I'm a beginner / I'm already advanced — is it for me?
Both. The course starts with structure and strategy, then teaches each question type in its own short lesson with a model answer. Beginners get a clear path; advanced students can jump straight to the tasks costing them points.
Where do I take the course?
The course is hosted on the Aidoss Academy website and works on phone, tablet, or laptop. Study at your own pace and receive course updates.
What exactly do I get?
Self-Study includes the full website course, 16 Module-End Skill Tests, lesson PDFs, audio, companion and premium course resources, vocabulary and grammar resources, course updates, basic technical support, and a $25 credit toward separately sold Complete Timed Mock Exams or Aidoss Library resources.
Which tests are included?
The website course includes 16 Module-End Skill Tests: four each for Reading, Listening, Speaking, and Writing. The 20 Complete Timed Mock Exams are separate purchases. Self-Study includes a $25 credit toward those complete exams or eligible Aidoss Library resources.
Do I get personal feedback on Speaking and Writing?
No. Self-Study is independent study with model answers. Practice Plus adds teacher correction, and Guided Sprint adds private 1:1 checkpoints.
